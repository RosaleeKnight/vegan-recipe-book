Ingredients
  • 4 Bananas
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 4 plant-based sausages
  • 2 Tsp Margarine
  • Pinch of black pepper
Instructions
  1. Slice bananas length wise, cut tomato in half, cut plant-based sausages in half.
  2. Add 2 tsp of margarine into a frying pan on medium heat.
  3. Fry bananas 3 minutes on one side then turn and fry 3 minutes on the other.
  4. Bananas should still feel firm. Set on the plate.
  5. Using the liquid left over fry sausages and tomatoes cut side down and for 3 minutes then turn and cook for another 3 minutes on the other side.
  6. Adding a pinch of black pepper to the tomato when itâ€™s turned over.
  7. Tomatoes should be soft all the way through and the sausages crispy. Serve.
