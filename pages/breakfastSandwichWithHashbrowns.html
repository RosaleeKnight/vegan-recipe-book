Ingredients
  • 150g plant-based sausage.
  • 4 plant-based eggs slices
  • 4 slices non-dairy Cheddar cheese
  • 4 English muffins halved.
  • 4 tablespoons margarine.
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 100g Hashbrowns
Instructions
  1. Cook sausages in a large, non-stick sprayed skillet over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes a side, or until golden brown and crisp.
  2. Fry plant-based egg patties 3 minutes on both sides until lightly crispy.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk margarine and maple syrup until creamy and smooth. Toast the English Muffins.
  4. Spread 1 tablespoon of maple spread on English Muffin, both halves.
  5. Place 1 egg on the bottom of an English muffin then top with cooked sausage and slice of cheese.
  6. Microwave for 30-60 seconds to melt cheese then cap with English muffin top.
  7. Continue making other sandwiches.
  8. Fry hashbrowns over medium heat for 10 minutes and serve with sandwiches.
