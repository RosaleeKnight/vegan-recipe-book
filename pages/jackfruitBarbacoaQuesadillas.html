All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

2 Tbsp grape seed oil

1/2 medium yellow onion chopped

2 cans of green Jackfruit drained and pat dried

3 cloves minced garlic

2-3 Tbsp coconut sugar or organic brown sugar

1/2 tsp each salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

2 tsp ground smoked paprika

2 1/2 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp ground coriander powder

2 tsp dried oregano

3 whole chipotle peppers in adobo sauce

2 Tbsp tamari sauce

Juice of 1 med lime

1/3 cup water

6 flour tortillas

2 cups or vegan cheddar cheese

1 cup of spinach

Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.

2. Once hot, add oil, onion, and garlic.

3. Saut√© for 4-5 minutes until onions and garlic are soft.

4. Add the pat dried jackfruit by shredding jackfruit with fork.

5. Cook for 5 min by stirring the mixture frequently.

6. Add coconut sugar, salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, coriander, oregano, chipotle peppers + adobo sauce, tamari, lime juice, and water. Stir to combine.

6. Cover and cook over medium-low heat for at least 20 minutes until Jackfruit is tende, the color has deepened and there is no more liquid left. Stir occasionally.

7. Taste and adjust flavor as needed. Set aside.

8. Warm up the flour tortillas and fill them with vegan cheese, Jackfruit barbacoa, few spinach leaves, add more vegan cheese.

9. Cover with another tortilla and cook the quesadillas over a medium heat on a non stick pan.

10. Repeat the process with other tortillas.

11. Enjoy with fresh guacamole and salsa.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
