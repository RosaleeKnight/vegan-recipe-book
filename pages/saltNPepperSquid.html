All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

4 king oyster mushrooms

3 cloves garlic

1 Thai chilli

3 spring onions

1 tsp Chinese five spice

1 tsp gochujaru (red chilli powder)

---For the marinade---

1 tbsp mirin

1 tbsp light soy sauce

1 tsp rice vinegar

1 nori sheet broken up

1 tsp white pepper

---For the dry batter---

3 tbsp corn starch

4 tbsp rice flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp white pepper

Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1. Start by cutting up your king oyster mushrooms into squid like shapes, I used a bottle cap to make rings and scored the other pieces.

2. Now mix together the marinade ingredients into a bowl and add in the mushrooms.

3. Mix together the dry batter ingredients and coat the mushrooms in the dry batter. Heat up some oil and fry your mushrooms until golden and crispy and set aside on a drying rack to let the oil drop off.

4. Now fry off the garlic chili and spring onion in a little bit of oil and then turn off the heat and add in the crispy mushrooms and toss to coat.

5. Toss the shrooms in the Chinese five spice and red chilli powder then serve with the green spring onions and enjoy!!
