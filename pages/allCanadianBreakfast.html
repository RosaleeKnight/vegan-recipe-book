Ingredients
  • 4 plant-based eggs
  • 4 sausages
  • 200g plant based Canadian bacon
  • 2 Hashbrown Patty
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Vegan margarine
  • Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tomato
Instructions
  1. Heat oven to 400F and bake hashbrown, sausage, bacon and eggs for 20 flipping at the 10-minute mark.
  2. Slice a whole tomato.
  3. Toast bread adding margarine and peanut butter to taste.
