All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

2 whole roasted peppers

1 onion diced

1 can kidney beans

spices to taste

---Vegan Cheese Sauce---

1 cup cooked white beans, such as cannelini

1/2 cup plant milk of choice, preferably unsweetened

1/3 cup vegan cheese shreds, OR 5 tbsp nutritional yeast

1/2 tsp salt

1/8 tsp garlic powder

If using nutritional yeast, add 1/2 tsp white or apple cider vinegar (can be omitted)

2 tsp vegan buttery spread or olive oil.

Instructions

1. Drain and rinse beans if they are canned.

2. Blend together all ingredients except cheese, either in a blender or with a hand blender.

3. Transfer to a pot, and add the cheese, if using.

4. Heat on low, stirring occasionally, until desired serving temperature is reached and optional cheese is melted.

5. Add more milk of choice if a thinner sauce is desired.

6. Fill some tortillas with roasted peppers, onion, kidney beans, vegan cheese sauce, and spices to taste.

7. Bake in the oven at 390° F (200° C) for about 10-15 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

The meal contains ingredients derived from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, legumes, and beans. It does not contain animal by products animal products, including red meat, poultry, fish, eggs, honey and dairy products such as whey.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
