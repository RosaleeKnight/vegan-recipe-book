All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

200g Plant-based chicken tenders

maple syrup for serving

hot sauce for serving

---Vegan Waffles---

2 tbsp ground flax

4 tbsp water

1¾ cups soy milk or another non-dairy milk

1 tsp white vinegar

1½ cup whole wheat pastry flour

⅓ cup brown sugar or coconut sugar

½ teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking powder

½ teaspoon baking soda

canola oil

Instructions

---Vegan Waffles---

1. Combine the flax and water in a large mixing bow to make the flax egg.

2. Pour the soy milk into a mixing bowl and add the vinegar. Set aside.

3. Combine the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in another mixing bowl.

4. Pour the flax egg into the soy milk and stir well.

5. Pour the liquid into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

6. Preheat the waffle iron and cover both sides with canola oil.

7. Ladle some batter onto the waffle iron (about ½-3/4 cup), and cook for about 3 minutes, or until the iron's indicator shows that they are done.

8. Complete with the remaining batter.

9. Keep finished waffles warm in a toaster oven or oven at 200° or stacked and covered with a clean kitchen towel.

---Vegan Chicken---

1. Bake in oven (follow bag instructions), serve hot over the waffles.

2. Top with maple syrup and hot sauce.

Frequently Asked Questions

The meal contains ingredients derived from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, legumes, and beans. It does not contain animal by products animal products, including red meat, poultry, fish, eggs, honey and dairy products such as whey.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
