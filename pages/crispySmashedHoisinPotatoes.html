Ingredients
  • Potatoes: 10-15 baby potatoes
  • Pepper: 1 chopped in cubes
  • Spring onions: 3 thinly chopped
  • Ginger: 1tbsp finely chopped
  • Red chilli: 1 sliced
  • Soya sauce or tamari: 1 tbsp
  • Sriracha: 1 tbsp
  • Hoisin sauce: 1 tbsp
  • Sesame oil: 1 tbsp
  • Vegetable oil for shallow frying
Instructions
  1. Boil baby potatoes in water till cooked, drain and allow to cool.
  2. Smash potatoes and fry in vegetable oil till the skin is crisp.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a pan and add ginger, spring onions and peppers.
  4. Cook for a few minutes and add soya sauce chilli and sriracha.
  5. Add smashed potatoes and stir in hoisin sauce.
  6. Mix well to combine.
