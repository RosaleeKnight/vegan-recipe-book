All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tbsp cumin seeds

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 small onion, diced

1 tbsp lemon juice

1/2 tsp tumeric powder

2 cups butternut squash

1/2 tsp ginger paste

1 can white kidney beans

1 tbsp tomato paste

1/2 tsp salt, to taste

4 cups of vegetable stock

2 tbsp corn starch mixed with veg stock from soup

1 tbsp coconut cream

Instructions

1. Fry a handful of sage with olive oil and salt in a pan.

2. Add oil, cumin seeds, garlic and ginger to a medium pot. Simmer for 5 minutes.

3. Add butternut squash and pan fry for 10 minutes mixing and cooking until squash is tender.

4. Add the rest of the ingredients except corn starch.

5. Take a cupful of veg stock from the pan and add your corn starch and mix.

6. Add to pot and bring to boil. Simmer for 10 mins Taste for salt and lemon.

7. Garnish with red chili flakes and sage

The meal contains ingredients derived from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, legumes, and beans. It does not contain animal by products animal products, including red meat, poultry, fish, eggs, honey and dairy products such as whey.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
