All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

4 bagels

1 jar of pizza sauce

1 1/2 tsp dried oregano

1 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (or more if you like it spicy!)

grated vegan parmesan

1/2 pack plant based pepperoni

Instructions

1. Position an oven rack about 6 inches from the broiler, and turn the broiler on high

2. Spread 1/4 cup pizza sauce on each bagel, drop the mozzarella on, and scatter pepperoni on top (I used 10 per bagel)

3. Place the bagels on a large broiler safe baking pan, spray the tops of the bagels with some spray oil and broil for about 6 minutes or until the cheese is nice and melted and the pepperoni have a delicious slight char to them, keep a very close eye because these beauties will turn rom charred deliciousness to burnt and inedible pretty quickly!

4. Sprinkle each one evenly with the dried oregano, red pepper flakes, vegan parmesan, and serve!

The meal contains ingredients derived from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, legumes, and beans. It does not contain animal by products animal products, including red meat, poultry, fish, eggs, honey and dairy products such as whey.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
