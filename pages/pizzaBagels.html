Ingredients
  • 4 bagels
  • 1 jar of pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (or more if you like it spicy!)
  • grated vegan parmesan
  • 1/2 pack plant based pepperoni
Instructions
  1. Position an oven rack about 6 inches from the broiler, and turn the broiler on high
  2. Spread 1/4 cup pizza sauce on each bagel, drop the mozzarella on, and scatter pepperoni on top (I used 10 per bagel)
  3. Place the bagels on a large broiler safe baking pan, spray the tops of the bagels with some spray oil and broil for about 6 minutes or until the cheese is nice and melted and the pepperoni have a delicious slight char to them, keep a very close eye because these beauties will turn from charred deliciousness to burnt and inedible pretty quickly!
  4. Sprinkle each one evenly with the dried oregano, red pepper flakes, and vegan parmesan if you please, and serve!
