Ingredients
  • 2 red peppers
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 sheet Nori, crumbled
  • 1/2 Tsp garlic powder
Instructions
  1. Cut red peppers in half, deseed and roast at 200C/400F for 30mins.
  2. Remove the skin.
  3. Mix and marinate 30-60mins.
  4. Use for sushi or other things.
