Ingredients

Tofu Marinade

  • 400g tofu
  • 3 big Tbsp peanut butter
  • juice of 1 lime
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 Tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 Tsp ginger powder
  • 1/2 Tsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • Maple Syrup
Instructions
  1. In a bowl mix peanut butter, lime, salt, chili powder, ginger powder, garlic powder, soy sauce.
  2. Pat dry and tear in chunks adding it to the mix fully coating it.
  3. Spread evenly on a lined baking tray and generously drizzle maple syrup.
  4. Bake at 175C/350F for 25min.
