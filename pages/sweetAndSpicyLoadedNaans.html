Ingredients
  • 2 vegan naan breads
  • 1 tin (400g) chickpeas
  • Half red onion sliced
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp mango chutney
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (½ for chickpeas, ½ for mayo)
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayo
  • 1 lime
  • Flaked almonds
  • chilli flakes
  • coriander
Instructions
  1. Heat a pan with a drizzle of oil, add drained chickpeas, garam masala, garlic powder & mango chutney and cook for 4-5 mins.
  2. Meanwhile, dampen your naans lightly with water & put under the grill for a few mins.
  3. Combine your mayo with garlic powder & a squeeze of lime.
  4. Layer naans with the garlic mayo, chickpeas, red onion, almonds, sprinkle of chilli flakes, coriander & more lime.
