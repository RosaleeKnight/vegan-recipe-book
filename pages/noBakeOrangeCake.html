All Vegan Recipe Book

Plant based dishes with no animal byproducts and drinks for all occassions.

Ingredients

---For the crust---

1/2 cup rolled oats

1/2 cup raw almonds

1/2 cup shredded coconut

5 large juicy medjool dates, pitted (if your dates are dry, soak them for 30 minutes in warm water)

Pinch of sea salt

1 tbsp of raw cacao powder

---For the orange layer---

1 can (400ml) of full fat coconut milk

1 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice (with pulp)

1 tbsp of lemon juice

1/4 cup (60ml) of maple syrup

1 tbsp of corn starch

1 tsp of agar agar

Instructions

1. To prepare the crust, add oats, almonds coconut and dates to a food processor.

2. Blitz until wet sand consistency.

3. Add salt and cacao powder, blend to incorporate.

4. Transfer to a 20cm cake form (line the bottom with baking paper) and press tightly to form an even layer.

5. Refrigerate.

6. Add all orange layer ingredients to a blender and blend shortly, until smooth.

7. Transfer to a saucepan and bring to a simmer, cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.

8. Let cool for about 20 minutes, pour over chilled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

9. Add the toppings of your choice and enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

The meal contains ingredients derived from plants, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, oils, whole grains, legumes, and beans. It does not contain animal by products animal products, including red meat, poultry, fish, eggs, honey and dairy products such as whey.

All recipes that show multiple items are meals that make 2 servings and single item recipes including drinks create 1 serving.

It's based off of two things, how filling the item is and how long it takes to prepare. Lunch contains moderately filling items that take less then 2hrs to prepare. Dinner contains very filling items which are protien heavy and dishes that take 2hrs or more to prepare (ex: holiday dishes).
