Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 5 large juicy medjool dates, pitted (if your dates are dry, soak them for 30 minutes in warm water)
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 1 tbsp of raw cacao powder

    • For the orange layer:

  • 1 can (400ml) of full fat coconut milk
  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice (with pulp)
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) of maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp of corn starch
  • 1 tsp of agar agar
    • Instructions
    1. To prepare the crust, add oats, almonds coconut and dates to a food processor.
    2. Blitz until wet sand consistency.
    3. Add salt and cacao powder, blend to incorporate.
    4. Transfer to a 20cm cake form (line the bottom with baking paper) and press tightly to form an even layer.
    5. Refrigerate.
    6. Add all orange layer ingredients to a blender and blend shortly, until smooth.
    7. Transfer to a saucepan and bring to a simmer, cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
    8. Let cool for about 20 minutes, pour over chilled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
    9. Add the toppings of your choice and enjoy!
