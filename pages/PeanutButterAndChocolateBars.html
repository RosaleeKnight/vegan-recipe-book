Ingredients
  • 230g creamy peanut butter ⁣
  • 100ml maple syrup⁣
  • 100g coconut flour⁣
  • 100g dark chocolate ⁣
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil ⁣
  • Flaky salt ⁣
Instructions
  1. In a bowl combine the peanut butter and maple syrup.
  2. Add the coconut flour. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Transfer the batter into a small dish lined with parchment paper. Press it down evenly with a spatula. Refrigerate for 10 mins.
  4. Melt the dark chocolate and coconut oil together.
  5. Pour melted chocolate on top of the peanut butter layer and spread evenly. ⁣
  6. Refrigerate until hardened. (Takes about 20-30 mins)⁣
  7. Finally cut it into slices and sprinkle flaky salt on top! (Store in the fridge)⁣
