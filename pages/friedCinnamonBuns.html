Ingredients
  • 4 square flat icing free vegan cinnamon buns
  • 2tsp margarine
  • 50g Maple Syrup
Instructions
  1. Cut the cinnamon bun in half cutting through the side separating the top from the bottom.
  2. Heat a frying pan to medium heat and add 2 tsp of margarine allowing it to melt.
  3. Set the cinnamon bun pieces cut side down into the pan and fry 2 minutes on each side until crispy.
  4. Remove from pan and plate pouring maple syrup on top.
