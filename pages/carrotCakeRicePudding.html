Ingredients

Carrot Cake Rice Pudding:

  • 1 cup rice (arborio or short grain is best)
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3 cups plant milk
  • 2 tbsp liquid sweetener
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp gingerbread spice mix or 1 tsp pumpkin spice
  • 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Handful of raisins
  • Handful of chopped walnuts

Cinnamon Ginger Nut Bliss Balls

  • 1/2 cup prunes (you can use dates)
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • Handful of walnuts
  • Handful of sunflower seeds
  • 3 tbsp plant milk
  • 1/4 cup almond meal
  • 2 tsp coconut flour
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • 2 tsp liquid sweetener
Instructions
  1. Bring your rice and water to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked.
  2. Add in your plant milk of choice, shredded carrot, vanilla extract, sweetener and spices and cook until the milk is almost absorbed.
  3. Add in raisins and walnuts,Mix a little milk with the cornstarch and pour it into the rice pudding, keep stirring on low heat until thick.
  4. Add all bliss ball ingredients into a blender, blend until smooth.
  5. Wet your hands, form balls, roll them in coconut flakes and done.
  6. Garnish the bowl with the bliss balls. Enjoy!
