Ingredients

Sausage:

  • 1 pack plant based sausage

French Toast:

  • 1 cup non-dairy milk
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 teaspoon ground cinnamon sugar
  • 3 teaspoon white sugar
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 6 slices bread
Instructions

French Toast

  1. Whisk together the non-dairy milk, flour, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Pour into a shallow dish, like a wide bowl or pie dish.
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat. Add some vegan butter or oil to the pan.
  4. I use about a tablespoon of butter and add more butter while cooking additional slices.
  5. Working with 2 bread slices at a time, dip each slice into the mixture on both sides.
  6. Once the pan is hot, cook each slice of French toast on both sides until golden brown.
  7. Repeat with remaining slices. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
