Ingredients
  • 1 pointed cabbage or Napa cabbage

Braising liquid:

  • 1/2 cup (125ml) orange juice
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) water
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • salt to taste
  • 1 Tsp each ground cumin
  • 1 Tsp ground coriander
  • 1 Tsp paprika
  • chili flakes

Tahini sauce:

  • 4 Tbsp tahini
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tsp sriracha
  • salt to taste
  • water to thin
Instructions
  1. Cut cabbage into quarters and fry each side in a little oil until charred.
  2. Mix orange juice, water, maple syrup, salt, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground paprika, chili flakes and add mix to the pan.
  3. Let it cook for about 10mins until the broth is gone.
  4. Mix tahini, maple syrup, lemon juice, sriracha and water. Serve over the cabbage.
