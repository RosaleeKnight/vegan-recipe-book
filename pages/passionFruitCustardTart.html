Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1/2 cup raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 2 tbsp of coconut oil
  • 3 tbsp of maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp of cold water

For the filling:

  • 1 cup (240ml) of full fat coconut milk, canned
  • Seeds and juice from 4-5 passion fruits (depending on size)
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp of thick coconut yoghurt OR vegan cream cheese
  • 1/2 tsp of agar agar
Instructions
  1. Add almonds, cashews, coconut, oats and salt to a food processor.
  2. Blitz until rough flour consistency.
  3. While still blending, slowly add coconut oil, maple syrup and water.
  4. Transfer the dough into lightly greased 20cm tart pan with removable bottom, form the crust by pressing with your fingers.
  5. Bake at 175C/350F for 12 minutes, then leave to cool on a cooling rack.
  6. Blend milk and passion fruit flesh and passing it through a sieve.
  7. Add coconut milk, passion fruit flesh, corn starch and maple syrup to a saucepan.
  8. Whisk well and bring to a simmer.
  9. Cook gently for about 5 minutes, whisking continously, until the mixture is thick and creamy.
  10. Remove from heat and whisk in 2 tbsp of thick coconut yoghurt or cream cheese.
  11. Pour the filling into the crust, let cool to room temperature.
  12. Refrigerate for 6 hours or overnight.
  13. Decorate with passion fruit and enjoy!
